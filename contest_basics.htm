The experiment consists of a series of contests.

In a contest, two players compete by investing tokens on a wheel of fortune.

The winner of a contest earns tokens or receives an opportunity.

The more you invest relative to your opponents,

the bigger is your share on the wheel,

the higher is your probability to win.

Assume your opponent invested 250 tokens

Move the slider inside to consider investment levels for you.

Move the slider inside to consider hypothetical investment levels for the opponent

In the experiment, opponent will be an actual player and players will not know each others' investments.
Yet, considering other players' hypothetical decisions will help you make a better decision.
