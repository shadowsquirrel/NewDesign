We will now introduce leaders' competiton mechanics and see how the power ratio can have a significant effect on a leader's chance to win.

OK

A weak follower is less likely to win IN-group Contest and become the new leader of its group.

Therefore, a weak follower may benefit more when their group wins the OUT-group contest and each follower receives 100 tokens.

As a result, a weak follower may be more inclined to help its leader.

OK

On the other hand, a strong follower is more likely to win the IN-group contest and become the new leader of its group.

Therefore, a strong follower may benefit more when their group loses the OUT-group contest I and the followers get the chance to be the new leader of their group in their IN-group contest.

As a result, a strong follower may be more inclined to sabotage its leader.
