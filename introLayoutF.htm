YOUR DECISION

The section above is your main decision slider where you will make your help/sabotage decision during the game.

Drag the slider left to sabotage or right to help your leader.

Please chose a value inside the slider.

You will have limited time to make your decision.

Time left will be displayed on the right corner of your screen

If you are done before the time expires, you can click the Done button.

If you fail to submit within the time limit, your current entry will be considered as your final decision.

To make your decision, you need to consider what the others will possibly play.

But, while you make your decision, you will not know other players' decisions.

We provide you with a calculator to consider other players' potential decisions.

Click Show Calculator button below.

On the Calculator below, the green-boxed slider () represents your decision.

NOTICE

All the followers' sliders are set to 100 sabotage.

When you turn on the calculator,

All the other followers' hypothetical decisions will be set to

the current value on your decision slider.

Leaders' hypothetical decisions will always be set to 200.

Don't forget

The calculator is provided only to facilitate your decision making.

Only the decision you make in either decision sliders will have an effect on the game.

Get familiar with the calculator.

Click the done button when you are ready.
